About Barde

Barde is a multiplatform application for instrumental audio streaming. Our service is aimed at professionals in the tertiary sector but the application will also be available to the general public. The user of Barde will be able in a few clicks to create his musical atmosphere in the style of his choice and Barde will be responsible for generating it indefinitely.

Simple

Enjoy a specific song adapted to the situation while focusing on those who surround your environment !

Modulable

You can choose from the musical style to the instruments or the rythm to compose your own music

Hearty

Barde allows you to focus on a place, a dialog or a cool landscape. Enjoy a song adapted to any situation!
